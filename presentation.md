# UNO Amateur Radio Club
## Club Overview & PARC Funding Request

**ARRL-Affiliated Student Radio Club**
University of New Orleans

---

## About UNO ARC

- **ARRL-affiliated** radio club seeking official university recognition
- Founded with **9 students** at the first meeting
- **4 officers** elected at founding
- Advisors:
  - **Dr. Denis Svechkarev (K8UA)** — also approved as Station Trustee
  - **Gabriel Gutierrez**

---

## Membership & Meetings

- **Official members:** Current UNO students only
- **Unofficial members:** Anyone is welcome to attend meetings
- **Meeting schedule:** Fourth Thursday of each month at 6:00 PM
- **Communication platform:** Discord

---

## Goals & Activities

- Conduct **hands-on activities** and **technical builds**
- Prepare students for **amateur radio licensing exams**
- Serve members with diverse interests:
  - Cybersecurity majors
  - Electrical & mechanical systems enthusiasts
  - Radio newcomers
